Welcome to Dance Talent Hunt!

This is a Platform to let loose your inner dancer. Be it the funk of Hip-Hop or the elegance of Ballet. Be it the energy of Zumba or the class of Contemporary. We're ready for you and your talent. All the best for Talent Hunt.
